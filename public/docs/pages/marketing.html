Songs go viral on social media when they are repeatedly used by top creators. When a major influencer with millions of followers uses your track in a dance, edit, or trend, their audience often copies and remixes the idea, creating a UGC snowball effect. This leads to:

  • Massive exposure and organic shares
  • Increased streams on Spotify, Apple Music, and other DSPs
  • Growth in fanbase and recognition worldwide
  • Higher chances of being picked up by editorial and algorithmic playlists
