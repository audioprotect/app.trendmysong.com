AGREEMENT TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use trendmysong.com (the “Platform”). By using our services, you agree to the practices described below.

Effective Date: September 12, 2025

Platform: Trend My Song (“TMS”, “Platform”, “we”, “our”, or “us”)

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identifiers: Email, username, password, real name, and profile details.
  • Usage Data: Device information, browser type, and interaction logs.
  • Uploads: Music files, images, and related metadata provided for distribution, copyright, or promotion.
  • Account Access: Login credentials (email and password) securely processed and stored in Google databases.
  • Cookies: Essential session cookies only. We do not use analytics, marketing, or tracking cookies.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Deliver, operate, and improve our services.
  • Distribute your music to partner platforms.
  • Register and manage copyright/Content ID services.
  • Communicate about your account, updates, or support.

We never sell your personal information.

3. Sharing with Third Parties

  • Payments: Handled entirely by our Merchant of Record (MoR) via Stripe. We do not store card details.
  • Music Platforms: Audio files and metadata may be shared with third-party services (e.g., YouTube, Spotify, TikTok) for distribution or copyright purposes.
  • No Ads: We do not share your data with advertisers.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We keep data only as long as necessary to provide our services. If you request deletion, all personal info, uploads, and metadata will be permanently erased.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your information.

6. Security

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit.
  • Secure password storage and authentication practices.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 (or the minimum digital consent age in your region). If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for secure login sessions. These cannot be disabled as they are necessary for platform functionality.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will appear on this page with a new “Effective Date.” If changes are significant, we will notify you by email or within the Platform before they take effect.
