Step 1: Create Your Plan

  1. Visit app.trendmysong.com/pricing
  2. Customize a plan that fits your needs
  3. Click continue to proceed

Note: You can check out Flexible Pricing Explained to better understand our pricing model and perfectly customize your plan during checkout.

Step 2: Make Payment

  1. You will be redirected to a secure checkout page with your order details
  2. You can pay instantly, or use the payment link sent to your email to pay later
  3. For more info about accpeted payment methods, you may take a look here: Accpeted Payment Methods

Step 3: After Payment

  1. An auto-generated unique license key will be shown (example: XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX)
  2. You will also receive an email containing your order confirmation and license key
  3. Optional: Reply to the email with your license key — this helps our team verify and activate your account faster
  4. Even if you don’t reply, accounts are always activated within 4 – 8 hours

Step 4: Account Activation

  1. Our team will verify your license key and activate your account within 4 – 8 hours
  2. You will be notified once your account is active
  3. After activation, log in at app.trendmysong.com

Emails You May Receive

During the signup process, you may receive the following emails from support@trendmysong.com in this order:

  1. [Trend My Song] Application Received – Next Steps
    Sent instantly when you submit a customized plan at app.trendmysong.com/pricing. This email includes your plan details (total price, total tracks, services, etc.) and a payment link. It contains a "Pay Now" button if you prefer to pay later. If you already paid at checkout, no further action is required.
  2. [Trend My Song] Payment Received – Final Step: Verify Your License Key!
    Sent instantly after completing payment (either at checkout or via the email link). You only need to reply with your license key to speed up verification. Even if you don’t reply, your account will still be activated within 4–8 hours.
  3. [Trend My Song] Your Account is Now Active!
    Sent once our team activates your account. At this stage, you can log in to the portal at app.trendmysong.com. This is the last automated email you’ll receive for signup. From now on, all emails will be service updates and support tickets.
