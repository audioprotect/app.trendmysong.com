Introduction

Copyright protection ensures that your music is safe from unauthorized use across digital platforms. TMS registers your tracks in major Content ID and audio fingerprint databases, giving you worldwide protection and monetization opportunities.

What is Content ID?

Content ID is a technology that identifies copyrighted audio across online platforms by comparing uploaded content to a global database of registered works. Once your track is registered, it is automatically detected when used in videos, streams, or posts — ensuring you remain in control.

Platforms Covered

TMS provides copyright protection across multiple platforms:

  • YouTube Content ID – Protect and monetize your music across long-form videos and Shorts
  • TikTok Rights Management – Detect and monetize your music in short-form viral content
  • Meta Rights Manager – Identify and monetize your music on Instagram and Facebook
  • Streaming DSPs – Ensure your metadata is accurate across Spotify, Apple Music, Deezer, Amazon Music, and 150+ DSPs
  • Third-Party Fingerprint Databases – Register with global systems for additional protection

Fingerprint Databases

Benefits of Content ID Registration

  • Monetize usage of your music on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and other platforms
  • Block or remove unauthorized uploads when necessary
  • Track usage data to understand where and how your music is being used
  • Redirect revenue from unlicensed uses directly to your account
  • Protect your catalog against copyright theft or false claims

Prohibited Content

To maintain integrity, certain types of content cannot be registered in Content ID or fingerprint databases:

  • Public domain music
  • Royalty-free or stock music libraries
  • Sleep, meditation, yoga, and ambient noise tracks
  • Generic sound effects or loops
  • Tracks already registered by another distributor
