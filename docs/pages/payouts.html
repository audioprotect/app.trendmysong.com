Available Payment Methods

TMS ensures that artists and partners can easily receive their royalties and revenues through multiple global payout options. We support flexible withdrawal methods to suit the needs of independent creators, labels, and enterprise clients worldwide.

  • Bank Transfer – Ideal for direct payments to your bank account. Supported internationally with varying processing times depending on your country and bank.
  • PayPal – A fast and widely used payment method that allows instant global transfers directly to your PayPal wallet.
  • Stablecoins (USDT, USDC) – For crypto-friendly clients, we provide payouts on multiple networks (ERC-20, TRC-20, BEP-20). Stablecoin payments are usually processed faster and with lower transaction costs.
  • Other Options – Additional methods may be arranged for enterprise clients, including local payment processors or custom agreements.

Minimum Withdrawal Amount

The minimum withdrawal threshold depends on the chosen payout method:

  • Bank Transfer: usually $100 USD minimum.
  • PayPal: minimum $50 USD.
  • Stablecoins: minimum $50 USD equivalent.

Fees

Withdrawal fees vary depending on the method selected:

  • Bank Transfers: standard banking fees and SWIFT/SEPA charges may apply
  • PayPal: PayPal transaction fees and currency conversion charges may apply.
  • Stablecoins: a small blockchain network fee (gas/transaction fee) applies depending on the network used (ERC-20, TRC-20, BEP-20).

Processing Times

Processing times vary based on the selected payout method:

  • Bank Transfers: 2–7 business days, depending on your region and bank.
  • PayPal: usually processed within 24–48 hours.
  • Stablecoins: typically completed within a few hours once approved.

Important Notes

  • All withdrawals must meet the minimum threshold for the chosen payout method.
  • Fees are deducted from the withdrawal amount at the time of processing.
  • Processing times may be affected by weekends, bank holidays, or blockchain congestion.
  • Enterprise clients can request custom payout schedules and bulk settlement agreements.

At TMS, we aim to provide flexible and reliable payouts so that artists and partners can focus on creating and growing their music careers without financial barriers.
