Affiliate Program

Our affiliate program allows you to generate revenue by referring artists, labels, or creators to TMS. Each successful sign-up or purchase through your referral link earns you a commission.

Ways to Promote TMS

At TMS, we believe in growing together with creators, influencers, and marketing partners. If you would like to advertise TMS or collaborate with us, we provide several opportunities to earn while helping more artists discover our platform.

  • Content Creators – You can create videos, tutorials, blogs, or reviews about TMS and earn rewards through our affiliate program.
  • Influencers – If you have a strong social media presence (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, etc.), you can partner with us for sponsored promotions and collaborations.
  • Affiliate Marketing – Earn commission for every client you refer to TMS through your unique affiliate link.
  • Sponsorships – We offer sponsorship opportunities for influencers, agencies, and communities that align with our mission to support music creators worldwide.

Sponsorship Opportunities
